What is a hardfork?

A hard fork (or sometimes hardfork) is a radical change to the bitcoin protocol that makes previously invalid blocks/transactions valid (or vice-versa) this results in split and a new version of Bitcoin. For every original Bitcoin, you held you get one of these new bitcoins. Some have value and some do not.

To claim your new coins you have to split your blockchain. How to do this varies from fork to fork we will be adding a guide for each fork soon.
